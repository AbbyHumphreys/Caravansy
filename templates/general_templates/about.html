About Us

Caravansy is a small, friendly company with small origins. We started in a small town in the North Coast of Wales and have grown to serve customers needs around the UK and Europe.

We delight at meeting our customers needs and know that a caravan needs to be as special as your own home.

At Caravansy, we look for new, innovative ways to sell your caravan. Let's us do the work for you, matching buyers with sellers in unique, convienient ways.
